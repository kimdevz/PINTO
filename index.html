Why Emotional Intelligence
Parenting?

Emotionally intelligent parents positively manage their own emotions whilst confidently supporting their child's 'big' emotions, such as frustration, anger, and anxiety. The result is a more calm, connected home where life just starts to flow.

Emotional intelligence is fast becoming sought after in schools for both educators and their students. Research shows classrooms are calmer, run more smoothly and even record fewer behavior reports.

In short, Emotional Intelligence is the way forward for raising humans who are empathetic, self-aware, kind, and resilient.
